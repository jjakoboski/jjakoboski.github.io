About

Data scientist, program director, and technical project lead with 18+ years delivering impactful outcomes in oceanography, research project leadership, science communication, and stakeholder engagement.

Led the co-design and deployment of a multimillion-dollar crowd-sourced ocean observing network across Aotearoa New Zealand — including extensive stakeholder co-design, open data advocacy, and national-scale data visualisation for diverse users.

Combines MIT/WHOI Ph.D.-level analytical rigour with hands-on program delivery, stakeholder strategy, and data communication. Track record of forging partnerships across industry, government, and community lines, translating between technical and non-technical audiences, and driving open data culture change within traditional environments.
